Over the years Rich Harris and his company, Aero Finish, have restored many pieces of furniture in our home – including antiques from the early 1900’s we inherited from our grandparents.  His work is out of this world!  He is not only a craftsman, he is an artist with furniture and a genius at bringing out the greatest beauty in the wood.  He is also a good businessman:  punctual, reliable and honest.  He stands by his estimates and returns the furniture precisely when he says he will.   As long as Rich is in business, I will not go to anyone else for furniture refinishing and restoration.

Jennifer D.
